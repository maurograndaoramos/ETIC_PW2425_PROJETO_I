Our Story

Welcome to EDrive, a web app that lets you store your files and access them from anywhere. It was created by a team of developers composed by one person, who is a student at the ETIC_Algarve.

The app was created as a project for the Web Development course.

Features

  • Access from any device
  • User-friendly-ish interface
